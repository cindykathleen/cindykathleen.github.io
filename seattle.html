Seattle

March 28 - 31, 2017

I have always wanted to visit the beautiful city and I finally got the chance to do so! The trip consisted of the basic tourist traps and the "must try" foods of Seattle.

Some of the places that we got to visit were the Pike Place Market, the Gum Wall, Seattle Great Wheel, Space Needle, and Chihuly Garden and Glass.

A few of the MANY places that we ate at were Molly Moon's Homemade Ice Cream, Pike Place Chowder, Japonessa, Beecher's Handmade Cheese, Ellenos Real Greek Yogurt (which I still dream about sometimes..), and Elliott's Oyster House.
