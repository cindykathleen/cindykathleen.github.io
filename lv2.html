Las Vegas

February 23 - 25, 2018

♥ Happy 26th Birthday, Liv ♥

    • Started the weeked off with a roadtrip to Las Vegas with Melanie, Sam, and Sharon. Definitely dreaded the long drive but it ended up being 8 hours of McDonalds and High School Musical so I guess it wasn't too bad!

    Friday night's main event was RL Grime at XS, but we were all so tired that we left at 12am before RL Grime even came on.. So instead, we went to get Taco Bell at their Club Cantina location and walked around the strip.

    • On Saturday, we got brunch at Wicked Spoon, played golf at Top Golf, explored the hotel and got free mimosas, and got ready for the night. Saturday night's festivities was Illenium at Hakkasan. This time, we stayed the entire night and our table happened to be front and center!

    • Just like how the weekend started, the weekend ended with a long roadtrip home. Peace Out, Las Vegas.. can't wait to see you again soon!
