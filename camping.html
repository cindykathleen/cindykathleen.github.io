Camping

June 24 - 26, 2016

The first of many annual Sober Friends Outdoors Trip was a camping trip at Mt. Madonna County Park.

    • It was the first time camping for a few people in the group, and the rest of us weren't experienced campers either. We ended up having to borrow a lot of camping supplies (thanks Brandon) and setting up the tent took a lot longer than we'd care to admit.. it's actually embarrassing how long it took us (2 hours). Nonetheless, we still managed to get our tent and campsite up and running!

    We spent the weekend playing games, hiking, cooking and eating, and hanging out by the bonfire. I'll never forget hiking for hours just to get a small man made waterfall (it was pretty disappointing), and we ended up playing the bottle flip challenge because we were too tired to hike back. And how Alan woke us all up at 6:30am because he was sleeping talking, and some person or animal walked by our tent at 4am which freaked us all out. Even through all of the struggles, it was a memorable weekend with great people! I look forward to our next annual trip.
