Yosemite

July 7 - 9, 2017

This year, the annual Sober Friends Outdoors Trip took place in Yosemite.

We decided to hike the Upper Yosemite Falls Trail, which is about a 7.6 mile round trip with an elevation of 6,526 feet. This was definitely the toughest hike we have ever tried to do (even though we didn't quite make it all the way to the top), but the view was unbeatable and worth it.

During our down time in our Airbnb, we played board games and card games, cornhole, video games, basketball, and ping pong. We also made a lot of homemade food like fried chicken and waffles, tacos, and sandwiches.
