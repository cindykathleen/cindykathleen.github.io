New York

March 23 - 27, 2018

I left my heart in New York City

I planned the trip to NYC to visit my brother and best friend, Sarah! Since everyone was busy for a majority of the time I was there, I got a lot of time to explore the city by myself. It was the first time I ever explored a new place by myself and it was a great opportunity for me.

My brother's apartment is right by Times Square so I made sure to visit at least once a day. I'm happy I got to see it in the day time and night time to experience the difference. Other than Times Square, I explored SoHo, DUMBO, and the World Trade Center. I also got a chance to see some of the famous New York pop ups like the Macy's Flower Show, and Northern White Rhino statue. And OF COURSE I had to go to SoulCycle since it started in NYC!

    • I'm pretty convinced that New York City has the best food in the country. Some of the places that I was lucky enough to try were Red Rooster (thanks BuzzFeed's Worth It), Burger & Lobster, Maharlika, Turntable Chicken Jazz, Taco Dumbo, Dough, and The Press Lounge. Since my brother lived right next to Shake Shack, I got it 3 times and I was only there for 5 days.. Some of the desserts that I tried were Insomnia Cookies, Schmackary's, Magnolia Bakery, and Laduree macarons.

    • This trip really opened my eyes to the New York lifestyle, and I fell in love. After I graduate, I hope to get a job and move to NYC for at least a couple of years to get out of my comfort zone and experience new things. Here's hoping that I can make my dreams come true!
