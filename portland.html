Portland

January 19 - 23, 2017

We have been wanting to travel together for so long and when we saw $47 tickets to Portland, we simply couldn't pass that up.

Luckily, we found the CUTEST Airbnb that was located in the perfect location. We were able to walk to a lot of restaurants, bars, and cute shopping neighborhoods.

The main goal for our trip was FOOD (and tax free shopping but there aren't any good pictures of that). The memorable restaurants were Pok Pok (AMAZING wings), Portland Cider House, Salt & Straw, Pine State Biscuits, the Waffle Window, Screen Door, Wiz Bang Bar, and Apizza Scholls. And of course when you're in Portland, you have to bring a box of doughnuts home from either Voodoo or Blue Star.. or both!

We also took a walking tour of Portland and learned about the history of the city. I'm going to miss the weird and hipster vibes of this wonderful city, and I can't wait to go back for more!
