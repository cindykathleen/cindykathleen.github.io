Los Angeles

January 4 - 7, 2018

Girls Trip #2: another vacation with my mom, Sarah, and her mom!

My mom hasn't been to Disneyland in YEARS so we wanted to take her since no one can ever have enough Disney in their life. We were there from 8am to 10pm and left the park exhausted but extremely fulfilled since we rode every ride we wanted to and saw amazing shows: the parade, Mickey and the Magical Map, and Fantasmic. Disneyland truly is the Happiest Place on Earth!

Some of the places that we ate at were: Kang Ho Dong Baekjeong, Roscoe's House of Chicken & Waffles, The Pie Hole, Daikokuya, Chengdu Taste, and Porto's Bakery & Cafe. After another successful girls trip, we're already planning our next!
