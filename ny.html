New York

June 28 - July 04, 2016

"In New York, concrete jungle, wet dream tomato, there's nothing you can't do.." - Alicia Keys

My brother recently moved to New York this year for his company, Pinterest, so my mom and I decided to pay him a little visit since we have never been to New York before.. hooray for free housing!! Since my brother had to work, my mom and I decided to play tourists by ourselves and experience the city that never sleeps.

We went to some of the biggest Manhattan Attractions: Rockefeller Center and Top of the Rock, Grand Central Terminal, World Trade Center Site, Times Square, Flat Iron, Washington Square Park, St. Patrick's Cathedral, Brooklyn Bridge, Battery Park, the Met, and Central Park.

    • Whenever I scroll through my timeline of Facebook, there are always so many videos featuring the delicious and creative foods that are offered in New York. I went in with a HUGE list of places that I wanted to try. Unfortunately, I didn't have enough time or stomach space to finish my bucket list, but everything that I was lucky enough to try was amazing! Don't worry, I'll be going back again to finish everything, even if the list seems to keep getting longer and longer..

    • See you again in March 2018, New York
