HOW MY SOULCYCLE STORY STARTED

I have never been a fan of exercising. I would try to make it a habit, but one or two lazy days would set me back to the beginning. Even though I knew I needed to exercise to maintain a more healthy lifestyle, I never had the passion or drive to do it.

I have been hearing about SoulCylcle for a while throughout the years, but I never got around to trying it out. Then one day (1/13/18), Liv texted our group chat to see if anyone wanted to try it out with her. Leona and I expressed interest right away and we saw that there was a "BOGO" deal for first time riders. We bought our passes and started pumping each other up by signing up for our first class and learning everything there is to know about SoulCycle.

After attending our first class on January 15, 2018, we were hooked.

WHY SOULCYCLE?

To me, SoulCycle is more than just another workout class. It's the entire experience that they offer. It all starts on Monday at noon when you excitedly reserve your bike for the following week. Then the excitement continues to follow you all week until you open the door to one of their studios and get hit with the John Adler Grapefruit scented candle. Each studio is more aesthetically pleasing than the next with their clean, bright, and modern look.

I finally understood what it meant to be passionate about working out.
