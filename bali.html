Bali Islands

May 29 - June 2, 2014

My mom, aunt, cousin, and I decided to take a short girls trip to Bali, Indonesia through a Taiwanese tour. Throughout the trip, we participated in rafting, ATVing, a coffee tasting tour, the zoo, snorkerling, massages, and exploring the markets. It was great experience learning about their culture and spend time with my family.
